Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about our shipping processes and return policies.

Shipping Information

Processing Times

We take pride in carefully preparing your artwork for shipment:

  • Prints: 2-3 business days
  • Original Artwork: 3-5 business days
  • Custom Orders: Varies based on the project (you'll receive estimated timeframes)

Shipping Methods & Timeframes

We offer the following shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (domestic)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days (domestic)
  • International Shipping: 10-21 business days (varies by country)

Please note that shipping times are estimates and can vary depending on your location and customs processing for international orders.

Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated based on package dimensions, weight, and destination:

  • Domestic Standard: From $12.95
  • Domestic Express: From $24.95
  • International: From $39.95

Free standard shipping is available for orders over $150 (domestic) and $300 (international).

Packaging

We take exceptional care in packaging your artwork to ensure it reaches you in perfect condition:

  • Original paintings are wrapped in acid-free paper and bubble wrap
  • Prints are shipped flat in rigid mailers or rolled in sturdy tubes (depending on size)
  • All packages are clearly marked as containing fragile artwork

Tracking & Insurance

All shipments include tracking information that will be emailed to you once your order ships. Orders over $100 automatically include insurance coverage for the full value of your purchase.

International Orders

Please be aware that international orders may be subject to:

  • Import duties and taxes levied by your country
  • Customs clearance delays
  • Additional documentation requirements

These fees and requirements are the responsibility of the customer and are not included in our shipping charges.

Returns & Refunds Policy

Return Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, you may return most items within 14 days of delivery for a refund or exchange, provided that:

  • The artwork is in its original condition
  • Original packaging is intact
  • You have proof of purchase

Please note: Custom commissions and special orders are not eligible for return unless damaged during shipping.

How to Return

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact us at returns@brushandbloom.com within 14 days of receiving your order
  2. Include your order number and reason for return
  3. Wait for our confirmation and return instructions
  4. Carefully package the artwork in its original packaging
  5. Ship to the address provided in the return instructions

Return Shipping

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to our error or damaged goods. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance for your protection.

Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, we will process your refund:

  • Refunds will be issued to the original payment method
  • Processing time is typically 5-10 business days
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error

Damaged Items

If your artwork arrives damaged:

  1. Notify us within 48 hours of receipt
  2. Provide clear photos of the damage
  3. Do not discard the original packaging

We will work promptly to replace the artwork or issue a full refund, including shipping costs.

Exchanges

If you wish to exchange your artwork for a different piece, please follow the return process and place a new order for the desired item. Due to the unique nature of our artwork, direct exchanges cannot be guaranteed, especially for original pieces.
