Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, ordering process, and more.

About Our Artwork

What types of art do you sell?

Brush and Bloom offers a curated collection of original paintings, limited edition prints, and digital artwork from emerging and established artists. Our collection includes various styles from contemporary to traditional, abstract to realistic.

Are your artworks original?

Yes! All paintings listed as "original" are one-of-a-kind pieces created by our featured artists. Our prints are produced in limited quantities and are always marked as such.

Do you offer custom artwork?

Some of our artists accept commissions for custom pieces. Please contact us with your specific requirements, and we'll connect you with suitable artists.

Ordering Process

How do I place an order?

Browse our collection, select your desired artwork, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to provide shipping information and payment details to complete your purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for larger purchases. All payments are processed securely.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be canceled within 24 hours of purchase. Please contact us immediately if you need to cancel. After this period, orders are typically processed for shipping and cannot be canceled.

Shipping & Delivery

How long will it take to receive my artwork?

For prints and smaller pieces, typical delivery time is 5-10 business days. Original artworks and larger pieces may take 2-3 weeks as they require special packaging. International shipping may take longer.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International customers may be subject to import duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

How are artworks packaged for shipping?

We take great care in packaging your artwork. Paintings are carefully wrapped in acid-free paper, protected with bubble wrap, and shipped in sturdy boxes or tubes as appropriate to ensure safe delivery.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 14 days of receipt. Please see our Shipping & Returns page for full details.

What if my artwork arrives damaged?

In the rare event that your artwork arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos of the damage. We'll work with you to resolve the issue promptly, either by replacing the artwork or providing a refund.

Care Instructions

How should I display and care for my artwork?

We recommend displaying artwork away from direct sunlight and sources of humidity. For paintings, avoid touching the surface. Dust frames and glass gently with a soft, dry cloth. Specific care instructions may be included with your purchase.

Still have questions?

Our team is here to help! Reach out to us through our contact form or email us at support@brushandbloom.com
